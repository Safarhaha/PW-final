About Himchuli Academy

Our History

Founded in 2059 BS, Himchuli Academy has been dedicated to quality education for over a decade. Located in the picturesque area of Bhaktapur, our institution has grown from a small school to a premier educational establishment.

Our Mission

At Himchuli Academy, our mission is to provide comprehensive, quality education that develops intellectual, physical, social, and ethical capabilities of our students.

Our Vision

We aim to create a learning environment that inspires and prepares students to become responsible global citizens capable of meeting the challenges of the rapidly changing world.

Our Values

  • Excellence: Striving for the highest standards in academic and personal development
  • Integrity: Promoting honesty, transparency, and ethical behavior
  • Respect: Fostering mutual respect among students, teachers, and staff
  • Innovation: Encouraging creative thinking and problem-solving abilities
  • Inclusivity: Creating an environment where diversity is celebrated
